Our Profile

Revou is a leading educational technology (EdTech) company based in Indonesia...

Our mission is to create a world-class learning experience that is accessible, relevant, and impactful. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to build a successful career in the digital economy, and we are committed to being the catalyst for that transformation.


Our Vision

To be the foremost catalyst in nurturing world-class digital talent that drives innovation and growth across industries.


Our Mission

To Democratize Quality Education: To make high-quality, industry-relevant tech education accessible to everyone, everywhere.
To Empower Careers: To equip our students with not just theoretical knowledge, but the practical, hands-on skills that employers are actively seeking.
To Foster a Learning Community: To build a supportive ecosystem of learners, mentors, and industry partners that fosters continuous growth and networking.
To Drive Industry Growth: To directly contribute to solving the digital talent shortage by supplying companies with skilled, job-ready professionals.

What We Offer

Revou's flagship products are its intensive bootcamps and mini bootcamps, which include:

Data Science Bootcamp: A comprehensive program teaching data analysis, visualization, machine learning, and more using real-world tools.
Software Engineering Bootcamp: Focused on full-stack development skills to create robust web applications.
Digital Marketing Mini Bootcamp: Covering essential skills in SEO, SEM, social media marketing, and analytics.

Our curriculum is developed in close collaboration with industry experts from top tech companies to ensure it remains at the forefront of market needs. The learning model is hybrid, combining live online classes with self-paced learning, and is built around hands-on projects that simulate real-world challenges.
